发生了什么/What Happened

2023年2月17日，由于服务器硬盘阵列发生阵列卡和多块硬盘故障，导致服务器无法运行，在经过服务提供商几十个小时的修复后，我们的数据不幸全部丢失。其中存在大量程序及其源码未进行备份，导致我们的多项服务全部无法继续运作，最终决定，即日起DevNetwork及其下属组织NyaPoint和Sibaken，正式解散。目前相关服务正在由原DevNetwork负责人自愿重建中.


On February 17, 2023, due to the failure of the array card and multiple hard disks in the hard disk array of the server, the server could not operate. After dozens of hours of repair by the service provider, unfortunately all our data was lost. There are a large number of programs and their source codes that have not been backed up, resulting in the inability of many of our services to continue to operate. The final decision is that DevNetwork and its subordinate organizations NyaPoint and Sibaken will officially dissolve from now on. At present, related services are being rebuilt voluntarily by the original DevNetwork leader.
